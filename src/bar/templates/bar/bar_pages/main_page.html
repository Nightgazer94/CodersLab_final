Welcome to The Bar

At The Bar, we believe every moment deserves a touch of excellence. From the carefully selected ingredients in our cocktails to the warm, inviting atmosphere, we strive to create experiences that linger long after the last sip. Whether you’re gathering with friends, celebrating a special occasion, or simply unwinding after a long day, our team is here to make every visit memorable.

What is The Bar?

The Bar is more than just a place to enjoy drinks—it’s a destination for connection and discovery. Our passion for craft cocktails drives us to blend classic techniques with innovative twists, sourcing fresh, seasonal produce and top-shelf spirits. Sit back in our cozy lounge or take a seat at the bar, where our expert mixologists will guide you through signature creations and timeless favorites, all served with a smile.

From the smoky warmth of our Smoked Citrus Old Fashioned to the bright, herbal notes of the Garden Gin Fizz, there’s something for every palate.
